Jurleo's 30th

About

From the 24th to the 26th of July, 30 hours of partying, celebration and fun as we gather to celebrate our favourite baddie — Jurleo's 30th!

For Jurleo, the best birthday gifts are those shared by friends. Expect fun activities hosted by friends of Jurleo throughout the day in & around the villa. For a closer look at the schedule, head to the programme tab.

Good to know:
Feel free to drop by anytime across the two dates from door open. If you're planning to stay the night, please RSVP your attendance and reach out to Justin to confirm your accommodation — slots are limited. He's also your guy for anything else about accommodations or any other questions (he won't bite).

As we approach the big day, you'll be invited to a WhatsApp channel with live updates on upcoming events and a live itinerary throughout!
