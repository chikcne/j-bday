The programme

Programme

30 hours of partying across three days. Everything to see, do, and dance to.

Day 1 — Fri 24 July

The chill night before

Doors officially open at 6pm for those opting to stay over from the first day.

The first night is more of a chill affair. Approach the open deck with open hearts and open minds as you mingle over barbecue and drinks.

Day 2 — Sat 25 July

The main event — if you only come for one day, make it this one

Doors officially open for all at 11am. Lunch and dinner will be catered.

Expect activities such as a guided manifes-ditation session in honour of Jurleo, led by Mika, with even more later in the day. There's a pool too — so bring your skimpiest, sexiest ensemble to take a refreshing dip.

As the sun sets — painting the sky a brilliant amber — so do the animals come out to play. Here's all the sweet tunes you can expect to groove to:

  • Jer Dee — one of the organisers of the Rolling Stones–featured church (@churchlovesu), all the way from the Philippines.
  • DJ Tofu (debut) — expect pussy popping pop (his words, not mine) as high-gloss hyperpop collides with dancefloor euphoria. There will be Charli XCX, there will be sweat, there will be a 10-magnitude mothercuntquake.
  • The birthday boy — his first-ever DJ set, right before the cake-cutting ceremony at midnight.
  • DJ Bustin' — a sophomore appearance to end the night with a sonic bang.

Day 3 — Sun 26 July

Dance through the night, my beautifuls

The last set ends right before 4am.

Check-out is at 12pm, so please make sure you're up and packed by then.

Good to know:
Feel free to drop by anytime across the two dates from door open. If you're planning to stay the night, please RSVP your attendance and reach out to Justin to confirm your accommodation — slots are limited. As the big day approaches, you'll be invited to a WhatsApp channel with live updates and a live itinerary throughout!
